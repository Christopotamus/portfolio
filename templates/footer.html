Who is this guy, anyway?

Hello, I'm Christopher Budd. I'm a web developer who specializes in dyanmic web applications written in Django. My web chops are completely self-taught; however, I have taken non-web programming classes in the past. I thoroughly enjoy the entire development process of a website from the first 'django_admin.py startproject' command to the last line of JavaScript.



How did it start?

It all started when my dad bought me a Q-Basic programming book. Our family computer was a i486SX(the one without the floating-pount unit). From the second I typed my first lines of Q-Basic I was hooked. I knew I wanted to program. It wasn't until I was stuck in a factory making Toilet Paper (it's true) that I really got motivated. I wrote my first game, clouds mobile (no longer available) that achieved dover 4000 downloads.
